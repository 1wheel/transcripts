We won several cornerstone InsitePro software deals. InsitePro was our risk related platform. We received a Letter of Award for $125 million for just the development component of the SDI. We won a $2.1 million data acquisition project with a repeat customer and we continue to transform Intermap into a leading provider of geospatial solutions. All in all some very big wins for the organization.

We have been talking for years about how our geospatial 3DBI based applications will make a difference for our customers. And how our SDI Orion software gives large organizations and governments the ability to customize, organize, and analyze geospatially. This quarter delivered all of the front’s turnaround.

First our risk based application called InsitePro had more significant wins this quarter than ever before by providing a worldwide flood model. Using our 30 year data we opened and entered new markets that Intermap has never sold into before. In Q2, Intermap announced an agreement with a leading global reassurance carrier to license InsitePro in Argentina, Peru, Columbia, and Chile.

The adoption of foreign insurance throughout South America is increasing and InsitePro is right in the middle of this emerging market. InsitePro will be delivered to underwriters in this Latin American countries so that they have the necessary software to evaluate specific locations one by one or thousands at a time using innovative and reliable risk models and analytics designed by Intermap.

InsitePro is a tool that analyzes the risk of different perils associated with a specific address. For instance when underwriting flood insurance each of the risk needs to be calculated against an acceptable flood model and then analyze this potential risk of being impacted by a flood or other peril. These models are developed using an elevation model and then combined with other geospatial data such as land classifications and water produced by a storm as well as previous claims data that the insurance company may have used.

Intermap builds its flood models using both its World 10 DEM data and in some cases ultra high precision Intermap 5 meter [ph] data and LIDAR data. Now all of these models are available through InsitePro. This allows the larger underwriters to expand our market share in emerging areas. Some of the underwriters use this tool for marketing purposes and some use it for specific underwriting, depending on where they are in the penetration curve for that specific market. The InsitePro is ideally suited for this use and is slowly gaining functionality that can really improve upon the outlook provided by the RiskMeter product with CoreLogic which is its main competitor in the marketplace.

InsitePro wins show more of how our strategy takes spatial data infrastructure and creates vertical applications. Our SDI capability that allows us to work with local enterprises and governments is core to our strategy for creating sustainable and profitable products in the future. We have identified over 2600 companies in just the U.S. that can use the results of our InsitePro Software.

Our next big news was the first win of an Orion SDI. The negotiation of the definitive terms of the award are in process and as stated in the press release, a minimum revenue of $125 million is scheduled to be recognize over just the first two years of the contract. And that's all our portion of the award. The fund, that’s us just Intermap wins the first $125 million. The final terms and scope of the projects are currently being negotiated and we have had personnel in country, there about four of the past seven weeks.

In addition to the development part of this program we have been asked to provide operation and maintenance of the system for the next 18 years after it has been developed. This is clearly a big win for Intermap. We will also be looking to develop custom and vertical applications to sustain our position with this SDI customer.

Products like InsitePro will help the local government organize, analyze the geospatial data. This customized development will provide Intermap with additional up sell revenue opportunities in the future. All in all it was a great quarter for Intermap to demonstrate these two key cornerstones of our Orion strategy. We are working diligently on more InsitePro wins and other Orion SDI contracts that we are optimistic we will close in the not so distant future.
