So you could just say, our product, our technology is just perfectly fit for removal of the heavy metals and this is why we have a lot of enquiries, orders and so on from these industries, from the power plants, from marine industries, from the mining industry, it all comes down to the same thing. Our technologies just very well fit to take out heavy metals from water.
