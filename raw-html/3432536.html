In the past the sales weren't great but now in Europe sell through is improving quite a lot. So in Europe inventory is now nearly back to its normal level. Back to materials, once again for materials with high price volatility we've axed all of our business units to take tighter controls on those.

We have initiated this inventory plan and there is going to be some time difference between the time we launched a plan and the time we see the results. Yes we do still have some inventories but those inventories are items with good competitiveness in the fourth quarter we have a tighter control on commodity items whose prices tend to fall more easily and for items that whose prices do not drop easily we have more infantry.
